Verify Permit Need

If any of your contractors are carrying out high-risk works,
they are required to request a permit through this online system.
High-risk works include, but are not limited to:
- General activities requiring the use of landlord areas
- Hot works
- Isolation of any life safety system, including sprinkler systems
- Live electrical work on landlord equipment
- Roof works
- Working from a height
If any of your contractors are working on Cabot Circus/Hammerson demise
and/or require access to an area controlled by Cabot Circus/Hammerson a
permit to work will be required.
If none of the above applies to you, you do not need to request a permit, please liase with the retailer directly. Please ensure you comply with these requirements to maintain safety and
regulatory standards within the shopping center.
