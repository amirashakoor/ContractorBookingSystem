Important Notice for Contractors

Please note that not everyone working on site is required to obtain a permit.
Permits are mandatory only for contractors performing tasks that are classified as high-risk.
Additionally, if you are working on landlord property or need access to an area
controlled by Cabot Circus/Hammerson, a permit will be required.
Ensuring the safety of our staff, contractors, and customers is our top priority,
and we appreciate your cooperation in adhering to these requirements.
